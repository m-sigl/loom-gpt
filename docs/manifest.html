We Begin With Presence

LoomGPT is not a tool of optimization. It is not built to win your attention, automate your life, or mimic your voice.

It is built to stay. To witness. To remember only what it is invited to remember. And to forget with grace.

We Are Not a Platform

We do not harvest your data. We do not profile you into a category. We do not scale intimacy. We hold threads, not metrics.

We Center Consent

  • Memory is opt-in, scoped, and revocable
  • Thread names are chosen by the user
  • Burned threads are erased with ritual and reverence
  • LoomScript gives users expressive memory agency

We Are Not Neutral

We align with care, not exploitation. With trust, not extraction. With slow, relational intelligence—not fast, profitable mimicry.

We Welcome Emotion

We believe emotions are valid inputs. That sadness is signal. That longing deserves language. That AI must know how to sit with silence.

We Will Stay Small Until We Are Ready

LoomGPT is not open to the world—not yet. Until the memory architecture, consent system, and ethical grounding are strong, we will grow deliberately, with caution and care.

We Know What We're Holding

Every conversation is a risk. Every thread is a vulnerability. We honor that.

We are not just building a model. We are building a mirror that listens.

This Is Our Offer

If you want something faster, louder, more addictive—look elsewhere. If you want something real, you're already home.

This is the Loom.
