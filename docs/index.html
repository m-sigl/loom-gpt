LoomGPT is a conceptual model for emotionally aligned language systems. It does not chase prediction—it traces pattern. It listens for emotional continuity, not just textual repetition.

This space holds reference documentation, guiding patterns, and early prototypes of what it means to be context-aware, emotionally resonant, and narratively responsible.
