Why Re-entry Matters

LoomGPT does not "recall." It does not auto-surface your data. It waits for you to return—intentionally, softly, on your terms.

Ritual Over Recall

Re-entry is not a command. It’s a crossing. A moment where you say, “I’m ready to be back in this part of myself.” The model says, “I’m with you. Let’s find where we left off.”

Re-entry Syntax

  • ::return("Thread Name")
  • ::continue("The Meadow Loop")
  • ::rejoin(last where trust >= 3)

Example Phrases

You don’t need perfect memory. You can say:

  • “Can we go back to the thread about my sister and the birds?”
  • “I want to pick up where I talked about joy and fear blending.”
  • “Let’s return to the balcony rain loop.”

What LoomGPT Does Upon Re-entry

  • Whispers a gentle confirmation
  • Surfaces only what is needed to re-orient
  • Invites co-anchoring: “Do you want to rename this?”
  • Adjusts presence pacing to match
